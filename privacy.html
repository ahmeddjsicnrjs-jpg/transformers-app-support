Privacy Policy

Transformers is an internal workforce and task-management application for authorized company employees.

Operator

LIMITED LIABILITY COMPANY "TRANSFORMERS" (Ukrainian: ТОВАРИСТВО З ОБМЕЖЕНОЮ ВІДПОВІДАЛЬНІСТЮ "ТРАНСФОРМЕРИ"), company code 38758238.

Data We Process

  • Employee profile data, including name, phone number, position, role, and internal employee identity.
  • Work task data, including vehicle information, VIN, assignment, deadlines, status, comments, and status history.
  • User-generated comments and optional photos attached to task comments.
  • Work performance data, including internal points, bonuses, completion statistics, and work-related summaries.
  • Device push tokens, notification records, read state, and delivery metadata.
  • Selected task and profile data cached on the device for offline access and later synchronization.

How We Use Data

We use this data to authenticate employees, apply role-based access control, assign and review work tasks, synchronize data between devices, support offline usage, and deliver operational push notifications.

Storage and Service Providers

Application data is stored in Supabase. Photos attached to comments are stored in Supabase Storage. Push notifications are delivered through Expo Notifications and platform notification services provided by Apple and Google.

These service providers process data only to provide the application infrastructure and delivery services. We require service providers to protect data consistently with this policy and applicable law.

Sharing

Data is not sold and is not used for advertising tracking. Access is limited to authorized users and administrators according to their role and internal company procedures.

Account and Data Requests

The app does not provide public account creation. Accounts are created and managed by the company administrator. To request correction, restriction, or deletion of company account data, contact the company administrator or email app@transformers.com.ua.

Data Retention and Deletion

Employee profiles and operational records are retained while the company account is active and for as long as required for legitimate operational, security, accounting, or legal purposes. When data is no longer required, it is deleted or anonymized. Task history, comments, and related photos may be retained as company business records where required by internal policy or law.

After a verified deletion request, data that is not subject to a continuing legal or operational retention requirement will be deleted or anonymized. Backup copies may remain for a limited period until the normal backup retention cycle completes.

Permissions and Consent

Camera, photo-library, and notification access are optional system permissions requested for photo attachments and operational notifications. Users can revoke these permissions at any time in iOS Settings. Revoking a permission disables the related optional feature but does not prevent access to core task-management functionality.

Security

Access requires authentication. Server-side database policies and role checks restrict data access. Some data may be cached locally on the device for offline operation.
